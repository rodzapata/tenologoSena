Pivijay is a municipality located in the Department of Magdalena, in northern Colombia. It is situated in the valley of the Magdalena River, about 75 kilometers southeast of the city of Santa Marta.

Pivijay has a population of approximately 70,000 people and is known for its agriculture, particularly for the cultivation of rice, corn, and other crops. The town also has a vibrant cultural scene, with annual festivals and events celebrating local traditions, music, and dance.

If you're planning to visit Pivijay or the surrounding region, there are several attractions worth seeing, including the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta mountain range, the Tayrona National Natural Park, and the historic city of Santa Marta.
